Fire staff

BO2 fire staff

The fire staff is a great wonder weapon that is powerful and easy to obtain. Although not as effective as the ice staff for high rounds, it is still useful for rounds reaching the 70s. It is also much safer than the Ice staff, having savior effects similar to the lightning staff.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fire staff parts
  • Fire gem
  • Fire disc

STEPS

  1. Open the door to the church. In the following round, a glowing plane will apear. Shoot down this plane to get the first part
  2. Turn on gen 6 and another part will be gifted as a reward
  3. Kill the panzer soldat on round 8 and it will drop another part
  4. obtain the fire disc in the church
  5. Go into the fire tunnel near gen 3 in the trenches and obtain the fire gem
  6. Go back into the mound and create your new fire staff!
