Ice Staff

BO2 ice staff

This is a guide for how to obtain the ice staff in bo2 or bo3. The ice staff is a supremely powerful wonder weapon that makes high rounds extremely easy.

INGREDIENTS

  • Trenches part
  • Gen 5 part
  • Church Part
  • Ice disc
  • Gramaphone disc
  • Ice gem

STEPS

  1. Retrieve the 1st ice staff piece while it is snowing in the trenches by digging up a dig spot.
  2. Make sure to get the ice staff disc in the bunker at gen 2
  3. Retrive the second ice staff piece in the main area of the map
  4. Retrive the 3rd ice staff piece in the churches area
  5. Go to the mound and open up the basement using the gramaphone player and gramaphone disc
  6. Go to the ice tunnel near gen 6 and open the portal to the crazy place
  7. Obtain the ice gem
  8. Go back to the mound and create your ice staff!
